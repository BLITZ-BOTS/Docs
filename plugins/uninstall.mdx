---
title: Uninstall A Plugin
description: 'Uninstall a plugin from your bot.'
---

There are 2 ways to uninstall a plugin, through the [Controller App](/installation/controller) or through the [Blitz CLI](/installation/cli).

## CLI Command

```bash Terminal
blitz uninstall <plugin_name>
```

<ParamField path="plugin_name" type="string" required>
  The name of the plugin
</ParamField>



## Controller App


<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit A Bot">
    Click on the pencil icon on one of your bot's cards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Plugins">
    Find the "Plugins" section and locate the plugin you want to uninstall.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Uninstall">
    Click the bin icon on the plugins card.
  </Step>
</Steps>
